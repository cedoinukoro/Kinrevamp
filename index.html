Kitesurfing is our passion

Welcome to Kite in Negros !

First Kitesurfing School, rental and shop in the Dumaguete region.

Established in 2015

based near Dumaguete, Philipinnes

Our school is equipped with the best and latest equipment available in the area

Kitesurf gear is renewed regularly to offer the safest and most enjoyable experience for the students to learn how to harness the wind.

where to find us?

Our shop is located next to Zamboanguita cockpit. During the high season, you will find us right there on the beach.

Depending on the wind conditions we may be in another spot so make sure to contact us before hand.
